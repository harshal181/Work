6. Write a SQL statement find the number of movies in which each actor has worked.
Ans:-select a.name,count(m.mid)
     from actor a
     join movie_Actor ma
     on a.act_id=ma.a_id
    join movie m 
    on ma.m_id=m.m_id
   group by a.name;



7. Write a query to find names of the actor along with the movie name they have worked in.
Ans:-select distinct a.name,m.mname
    from actor a
     join movie_actor ma
     on a.act_id=ma.a_id
     join movie m
     on ma.m_id=m.m_id




8. Write a SQL statement to name of the movie in which a non Indian actor has worked.
Ans:-select m.mname
from movie m
join movie_actor ma
on m.m_id=ma.m_id
join actor a
on ma.a_id=a.act_id
where a.nationality!="Indian";

9. Write a query to display names of all movies in which ‘Salman khan’ has worked.
Ans:-select  mname 
     from movie m
     left join movie_actor ma
     on m.m_id=ma.m_id
     left join actor a
     on ma.a_id=a.act_id
     where a.name="Salman Khan";

10. Write a query to find names of actors who worked in movie with genre ‘comedy’.
Ans:-select distinct a.name
     from actor a
     join movie_actor ma
     on a.act_id=ma.a_id
     join movie m
     on ma.m_id=m.m_id
     where m.genre="comedy";
